Data Management

Your data is stored locally on this device only. No information leaves your browser.

Privacy Information

What data is stored?

  • Your selected goals (maximum 3)
  • Your favourited cards
  • Your display preferences (font size)

Where is it stored?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage on this device only. Nothing is sent to any server or cloud service.

Who can access it?

Only you, on this device, in this browser. No one else has access to your data.

How to delete it?

Use the "Clear All Data" button above, or clear your browser's storage/cache. This action cannot be undone.

About

Digital Wellness Plan is a self-guided wellbeing tool designed for educators in Further Education and Higher Education.

This resource provides evidence-based strategies for managing digital wellbeing challenges common in teaching and support roles.

Version 1.0 | Created with care for educators who care for others.
