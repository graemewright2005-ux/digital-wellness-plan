Privacy & Your Data

Your privacy matters. Here's what you need to know about your data:

  • What data is stored: Your goals, favourites, and settings preferences.
  • Where it's stored: Locally in your browser's storage on this device only.
  • Who can access it: Only you, on this device. No one else can see your data.
  • How to delete it: Use the "Clear Data" buttons above, or clear your browser data.
  • What we don't do: We don't collect, transmit, track, or store any of your data on servers. No analytics. No tracking.

About

The Digital Wellness Plan is designed to help education professionals develop healthier relationships with technology. This resource provides evidence-based strategies for managing digital wellbeing in demanding roles.
